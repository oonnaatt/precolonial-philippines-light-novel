# DARK_ENTITIES.md

## Filipino Folklore Entities for The Shadow Sultan

### Agents of Sitan
1. **Sitan**  
   - **Abilities**: Crafts illusions and controls shadows.  
   - **Vulnerabilities**: Light-based magic can dispel his illusions.  
   - **Story Potential**: Can become a corrupting force for characters exploring their own darkness.

### Viscera Suckers
1. **Aswang**  
   - **Abilities**: Shape-shifting, draining life force.  
   - **Vulnerabilities**: Garlic, holy water, and a sharp, pointed object can repel them.  
   - **Story Potential**: Representing fears of betrayal and loss of humanity.

### Demons/Spirits
1. **Tiyanak**  
   - **Abilities**: Appears as a baby, lures unwary travelers.  
   - **Vulnerabilities**: Must be confronted with compassion; can be quelled by giving it a proper burial.  
   - **Story Potential**: A character could be haunted by loss, leading to a complex relationship.

### Malevolent Engkanto
1. **Kapre**  
   - **Abilities**: Enormous strength, can confuse with smoke.  
   - **Vulnerabilities**: Chains can bind them, and they often have a fear of smaller creatures.  
   - **Story Potential**: Can symbolize a struggle against an overwhelming force.

### Mythological Giants
1. **Buwisit**  
   - **Abilities**: Heightened strength and resilience; often possess elemental powers.  
   - **Vulnerabilities**: Heroes could exploit their arrogance.  
   - **Story Potential**: Could serve as both an obstacle and an ally in times of great need.

### Additional Entities
1. **Lamang**  
   - **Abilities**: Can heal others or cause harm, depending on mood.  
   - **Vulnerabilities**: Must be treated with respect and offerings.  
   - **Story Potential**: Exploring themes of forgiveness and redemption.

2. **Engkanto**  
   - **Abilities**: Can bestow gifts or curses.  
   - **Vulnerabilities**: Can be outsmarted and banished with rituals.  
   - **Story Potential**: Characters may navigate moral gray areas.

3. **Siyokoy**  
   - **Abilities**: Aquatic creatures capable of manipulating water.  
   - **Vulnerabilities**: Dry land weakens them significantly.  
   - **Story Potential**: Serve as guardians of ancient secrets or treasures.
